# Customer Health

![Power BI Customer Health Dashboard](../assets/Customer_Health.png)

## Overview

The Customer Health dashboard provides a comprehensive view of customer value distribution and engagement status across the customer base. It helps identify where revenue is generated, how customer value is distributed geographically, and which customer segments require attention.

The dashboard combines customer lifetime value segmentation with customer health stages to evaluate revenue concentration and potential retention risks.

## Business Questions

This dashboard helps answer:

- Which customer segments generate the highest revenue?
- How is customer value distributed across cities?
- What percentage of customers are actively engaged?
- Which customer groups are showing signs of churn risk?
- Where should retention efforts be prioritized?

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Total Customers | Total customer count in the dataset |
| Average Customer Lifetime Revenue | Average revenue generated by a customer during their lifetime |
| Average Customer Order Value | Average revenue per transaction |

## Customer Value Distribution Across Cities

The geographic map visualizes customer value concentration across Türkiye.

### Insights

- Revenue is heavily concentrated in major metropolitan areas.
- İstanbul contributes the largest share of total revenue.
- The top seven cities generate approximately **43% of total revenue**, indicating strong geographic concentration.
- Regional expansion opportunities may exist in lower-performing cities.

### Business Impact

Understanding geographic concentration allows targeted regional marketing campaigns and localized growth strategies.

## Revenue Distribution by Customer Value Segment

Customers are grouped into four value segments:

- Low Value
- Medium Value
- High Value
- VIP

### Insights

- Medium Value customers generate the largest share of revenue.
- High Value customers represent a smaller population but contribute significantly to overall revenue.
- VIP customers contribute a relatively small share of total revenue, suggesting an opportunity to expand this segment.

### Business Impact

Retention and upsell initiatives targeting Medium Value customers can generate the highest short-term revenue impact.

## Customer Health Distribution by Value Segment

Customers are classified into three health stages:

| Stage | Meaning |
|---------|----------|
| Active | Regularly purchasing customers |
| Warm | Customers showing reduced activity |
| At Risk | Customers with elevated churn risk |

### Insights

- Higher-value customers tend to have better health scores.
- VIP customers show the highest Active rate.
- Low-value customers exhibit the highest churn risk.
- Medium Value customers account for the largest revenue contribution and therefore require close monitoring.

### Business Impact

Customer health monitoring enables proactive retention campaigns before revenue is lost.

## Recommended Actions

### Short-Term

- Launch retention campaigns targeting At Risk customers.
- Focus engagement initiatives on Medium Value customers.
- Monitor declining activity in Warm segments.

### Long-Term

- Increase the share of High Value and VIP customers.
- Develop regional growth strategies outside the top revenue-generating cities.
- Implement predictive churn monitoring models.
