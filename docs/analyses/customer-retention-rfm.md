# Customer Retention & RFM Analysis

![Power BI Customer Health Dashboard](../assets/Customer_Retention_RFM_Analysis.png)

## Overview

The Customer Retention & RFM Analysis dashboard evaluates customer loyalty and retention using RFM segmentation principles.

RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis categorizes customers based on their purchasing behavior and identifies customers who are most likely to generate future revenue or churn.

## Business Questions

This dashboard helps answer:

- Which customer groups contribute the most revenue?
- How much revenue is currently at risk?
- Which customers should be prioritized for retention?
- How does revenue distribution differ across RFM segments?
- What is the relationship between customer recency and value?

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| At Risk Customers | Customers showing signs of disengagement |
| Active Customer Rate | Percentage of currently active customers |
| Revenue At Risk | Revenue associated with customers likely to churn |

## Revenue Concentration by Customer Recency

This visualization explores the relationship between customer value (CLV) and recency.

### Insights

- Customer value generally decreases as recency increases.
- Recent customers contribute disproportionately higher lifetime value.
- Champions and Loyal Customers exhibit the strongest revenue concentration.

### Business Impact

Recent customer engagement is a strong indicator of future revenue generation.

## RFM Segment Composition

Customers are divided into four key segments:

| Segment | Description |
|-----------|------------|
| Champions | Highly engaged, high-value customers |
| Loyal Customers | Consistently purchasing customers |
| Potential Loyalists | Customers likely to become loyal |
| At Risk | Customers with declining engagement |

### Insights

- Potential Loyalists represent the largest revenue share.
- Loyal Customers form a significant portion of recurring revenue.
- At Risk customers account for a substantial revenue base that may be lost without intervention.

### Business Impact

Moving Potential Loyalists into Loyal or Champion status represents a major growth opportunity.

## Revenue At Risk by Customer Value Segment

This chart highlights the amount of revenue potentially lost if At Risk customers churn.

### Insights

- Medium Value customers account for the largest portion of revenue at risk.
- Revenue risk is concentrated in segments with the largest customer populations.
- VIP customers represent minimal immediate revenue risk.

### Business Impact

Retention efforts targeting Medium Value customers offer the highest potential return on investment.

## Revenue Share by RFM Segment and Gender

This analysis compares revenue contribution across genders within each RFM segment.

### Insights

- Revenue distribution is relatively balanced across genders.
- No significant gender-specific revenue dependency is observed.
- Retention strategies can focus primarily on behavioral segmentation rather than demographic segmentation.

### Business Impact

Customer behavior is a stronger predictor of revenue than demographic attributes in this dataset.

## Recommended Actions

### Immediate Priorities

1. Re-engage At Risk customers through targeted campaigns.
2. Protect Medium Value customers from churn.
3. Convert Potential Loyalists into Loyal Customers.

### Strategic Priorities

1. Increase Champion customer share.
2. Implement predictive churn modeling.
3. Develop personalized loyalty programs.
4. Monitor customer health metrics continuously.
