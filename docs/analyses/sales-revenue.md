# Sales & Revenue Insights

![Power BI Dashboard](../assets/Sales_Revenue_Insights.png)

## Overview

The Sales & Revenue Insights dashboard provides a high-level view of sales performance, revenue trends, and future revenue projections across key cities. It helps stakeholders understand how sales evolve over time, identify seasonality patterns, and monitor the geographic distribution of revenue.

The dashboard combines historical sales analysis with revenue forecasting to support strategic planning and resource allocation decisions.

## Business Questions

This dashboard helps answer:

- How do sales volumes change throughout the year?
- What are the long-term revenue trends?
- Are there any signs of seasonality in customer purchasing behavior?
- Which cities are expected to contribute the most revenue in the future?
- How is revenue distributed geographically over time?

## Total Orders Over Time

This visualization tracks monthly order volumes across the reporting period.

### Insights

- Order volumes remain relatively stable during the first half of the year.
- A noticeable decline appears after mid-year, indicating potential seasonality effects.
- The lowest order volumes occur during the final quarter.
- Sales activity stabilizes again toward the end of the year.

### Business Impact

Understanding seasonal demand patterns helps optimize inventory management, workforce planning, and marketing campaigns.

## Sales Trend by Period

This chart illustrates the overall revenue trend over multiple years.

### Insights

- Revenue demonstrates strong growth between 2021 and 2023.
- Revenue peaks above 1.5 billion during the most successful periods.
- The final data point shows a significant decline, which may indicate an incomplete period or emerging market changes.
- Long-term growth remains positive despite short-term fluctuations.

### Business Impact

Monitoring long-term revenue growth supports forecasting accuracy and strategic planning initiatives.

## Predicted Revenue of Cities

This visualization presents the projected revenue contribution of major cities over time.

### Insights

- İstanbul consistently contributes the largest share of projected revenue.
- Ankara and İzmir remain important revenue-generating markets.
- Revenue shares remain relatively stable across the forecast horizon.
- Geographic concentration suggests continued reliance on major metropolitan areas.

### Business Impact

Forecasted city-level revenue enables targeted regional investments and localized marketing strategies.

## Key Findings

- Sales exhibit seasonal fluctuations throughout the year.
- Revenue has shown strong growth over recent periods.
- Major cities continue to dominate revenue generation.
- Revenue forecasts indicate stable market concentration across key regions.

## Recommended Actions

### Short-Term

- Investigate causes of declining order volume during later months.
- Align promotional campaigns with seasonal demand patterns.
- Monitor underperforming regions for growth opportunities.

### Long-Term

- Expand customer acquisition efforts in emerging cities.
- Improve forecasting models using additional external variables.
- Reduce dependency on a small number of high-performing cities.
