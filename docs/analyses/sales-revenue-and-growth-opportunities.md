# Sales & Revenue Insights

![Power BI Dashboard](../assets/sales-revenue-insights.png)

## Overview

The Sales & Revenue Insights dashboard provides a high-level view of sales performance, revenue trends, and future revenue projections across key cities. It helps stakeholders understand how sales evolve over time, identify seasonality patterns, and monitor the geographic distribution of revenue.

The dashboard combines historical sales analysis with revenue forecasting to support strategic planning and resource allocation decisions.

## Business Questions

This dashboard helps answer:

- How do sales volumes change throughout the year?
- What are the long-term revenue trends?
- Are there any signs of seasonality in customer purchasing behavior?
- Which cities are expected to contribute the most revenue in the future?
- How is revenue distributed geographically over time?

## Total Orders Over Time

This visualization tracks monthly order volumes across the reporting period.

### Insights

- Order volumes remain relatively stable during the first half of the year.
- A noticeable decline appears after mid-year, indicating potential seasonality effects.
- The lowest order volumes occur during the final quarter.
- Sales activity stabilizes again toward the end of the year.

### Business Impact

Understanding seasonal demand patterns helps optimize inventory management, workforce planning, and marketing campaigns.

## Sales Trend by Period

This chart illustrates the overall revenue trend over multiple years.

### Insights

- Revenue demonstrates strong growth between 2021 and 2023.
- Revenue peaks above 1.5 billion during the most successful periods.
- The final data point shows a significant decline, which may indicate an incomplete period or emerging market changes.
- Long-term growth remains positive despite short-term fluctuations.

### Business Impact

Monitoring long-term revenue growth supports forecasting accuracy and strategic planning initiatives.

## Predicted Revenue of Cities

This visualization presents the projected revenue contribution of major cities over time.

### Insights

- İstanbul consistently contributes the largest share of projected revenue.
- Ankara and İzmir remain important revenue-generating markets.
- Revenue shares remain relatively stable across the forecast horizon.
- Geographic concentration suggests continued reliance on major metropolitan areas.

### Business Impact

Forecasted city-level revenue enables targeted regional investments and localized marketing strategies.

## Key Findings

- Sales exhibit seasonal fluctuations throughout the year.
- Revenue has shown strong growth over recent periods.
- Major cities continue to dominate revenue generation.
- Revenue forecasts indicate stable market concentration across key regions.

## Recommended Actions

### Short-Term

- Investigate causes of declining order volume during later months.
- Align promotional campaigns with seasonal demand patterns.
- Monitor underperforming regions for growth opportunities.

### Long-Term

- Expand customer acquisition efforts in emerging cities.
- Improve forecasting models using additional external variables.
- Reduce dependency on a small number of high-performing cities.

# Customer Growth Opportunities

![Customer Growth Opportunities Dashboard](../assets/customer-growth-opportunities.png)

## Overview

The Customer Growth Opportunities dashboard focuses on identifying revenue expansion opportunities through cross-selling, customer retention, and customer value analysis.

The dashboard combines product affinity analysis, churn monitoring, and customer performance metrics to support customer growth strategies.

## Business Questions

This dashboard helps answer:

- Which products are frequently purchased together?
- What cross-selling opportunities exist?
- What percentage of customers are at risk of churn?
- Which customers contribute the most revenue?
- How can customer lifetime value be increased?

## Cross-Sell Product Recommendation

This section identifies products that are commonly purchased alongside a selected base product.

### Insights

- Several products consistently appear together in customer baskets.
- Cross-sell recommendations are generated based on purchase frequency and confidence scores.
- Product affinity patterns reveal opportunities for personalized recommendations.

### Business Impact

Cross-selling increases average basket value and improves customer lifetime value.

## Frequency Indicator

The gauge visualization displays how frequently the selected product combination occurs.

### Insights

- Higher frequencies indicate stronger purchasing relationships.
- Frequently purchased combinations provide stronger recommendation candidates.
- Product affinity can be leveraged in recommendation engines and promotional bundles.

### Business Impact

Businesses can prioritize highly frequent product combinations when designing campaigns and bundles.

## Sales Type Distribution

This chart compares revenue generated by different sales structures.

### Insights

- Multi-category purchases generate the highest revenue contribution.
- Customers purchasing across multiple categories tend to spend more.
- Single-category purchases contribute significantly less revenue.

### Business Impact

Encouraging customers to explore additional product categories can increase basket size and overall revenue.

## Customer Churn Analysis

Customers are categorized as either Active or Churned.

### Insights

- Approximately one-quarter of customers have churned.
- The majority of customers remain active.
- The churned customer segment represents a significant opportunity for recovery campaigns.

### Business Impact

Reducing churn directly improves revenue retention and customer lifetime value.

## Top 10 Customers

This table highlights the highest revenue-generating customers.

### Insights

- A small number of customers contribute a disproportionately large share of revenue.
- High-value customers should receive personalized retention and loyalty initiatives.
- Revenue concentration among top customers should be monitored to reduce dependency risk.

### Business Impact

Protecting key customer relationships helps maintain revenue stability and supports long-term growth.

## Key Findings

- Cross-selling opportunities exist across multiple product categories.
- Multi-category purchasing behavior drives higher revenue.
- Customer churn remains an important area for improvement.
- High-value customers represent critical revenue assets.

## Recommended Actions

### Short-Term

- Launch personalized cross-sell recommendation campaigns.
- Re-engage churned customers through targeted promotions.
- Reward top-performing customers with loyalty incentives.

### Long-Term

- Implement recommendation systems based on product affinity analysis.
- Develop churn prediction models to proactively retain customers.
- Increase customer lifetime value through personalized product journeys.
- Expand loyalty programs for high-value customer segments.
